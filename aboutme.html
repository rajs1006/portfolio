About Me

I am a keen learner and an explorer willing to push boundaries to seek knowledge. I facilitate a working experience of Machine Learning concepts (Supervised/Un-supervised/Reinforcement-learning) along with a demonstrated history in Full-Stack Development.

I believe that my educational background and experience in the field of Machine Learning combined with my software engineering work experience (total 9+ years) have prepared me to extend my skills in tackeling real world problems.

I started my career as a Software Engineer in 2011 and ever since it’s been a journey of learning and growing. Although my job designation stated Java Developer, my inquisitive nature and zeal to learn always pushed me to try new things so, I learnt Javascript, DevOps, Database and the list go on :). To explore further opportunities, I left my job at Deloitte, India, and moved to Germany to study Machine Learning.

I completed my Master’s in Cognitive Systems from TU Berlin while working as a student data scientist at Funke Mediengruppe. In 2020, I joined Resonanz Energy GmbH as a data scientist and was promoted to Head of DSc within two years. On Behalf of Resonanz, I participated in two data science competitions held by DataHub Ruhr in association with Netconnect Germany and received first prize for both.

Working as a Head of Department in a bootstrap startup like Resonanz allowed me to design, code and build products that added both technical and business value to the company. My role required me to Engage with stakeholders to understand the business use case and then collaborate with internal and external tech teams/companies to carry out the implementation. This whole experience of owning deliverables end to end taught me how to convert a project into a well-managed, scalable, and profitable product.
